INCIDENT / NEAR-MISS REPORT

Report No: INC-2026-0056
Date of Incident: 03-Jun-2026
Plant: Refinery Unit 2, Tank Farm Section B
Equipment Involved: V-2001A, T-3050 (adjacent)
Severity Classification: Near-Miss (No injury, no loss of containment)
Reference Standard: OISD-STD-105 (Work Permit System), OISD-GDN-168 (Emergency
Preparedness Plan)

DESCRIPTION OF EVENT:
During scheduled maintenance on P-101 (WO-2026-0341 follow-up check), a contractor
technician attempted to access the pump platform without a valid work permit. Area
supervisor intercepted the technician before any tool was used on equipment. No
equipment was touched, no safety systems were bypassed.

ROOT CAUSE ANALYSIS:
- Immediate cause: Contractor unaware of updated work permit requirement following
  shift handover.
- Underlying cause: Shift handover checklist did not explicitly flag active work
  permits to incoming contractor teams.
- Systemic gap: Contractor induction training does not sufficiently emphasize
  permit-to-work verification steps per OISD-STD-105.

CORRECTIVE ACTIONS:
1. Update shift handover checklist to include explicit active work permit list,
   visible to all incoming personnel. [Owner: Shift Operations Manager, Due: 15-Jun-2026]
2. Refresher training on OISD-STD-105 work permit procedures for all contractor
   personnel. [Owner: HSE Department, Due: 30-Jun-2026]
3. Install permit status board at platform access points. [Owner: Site Engineering,
   Due: 15-Jul-2026]

Reported By: J. Menon, Shift Supervisor
Reviewed By: HSE Manager
