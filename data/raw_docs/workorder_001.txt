MAINTENANCE WORK ORDER

Work Order No: WO-2026-0341
Date Issued: 12-Mar-2026
Plant: Refinery Unit 2, Process Area
Equipment Tag: P-101
Equipment Description: Centrifugal Feed Pump, Crude Distillation Unit
Priority: High
Assigned To: Mechanical Maintenance Team B
Reference Standard: OISD-STD-105 (Work Permit System)

PROBLEM DESCRIPTION:
Vibration levels on P-101 exceeded threshold during routine monitoring on 10-Mar-2026.
Recorded vibration: 7.8 mm/s RMS against alarm limit of 4.5 mm/s RMS. Bearing housing
temperature reading elevated at 78°C, 15°C above baseline. Operator log noted unusual
noise from pump coupling during shift change.

WORK REQUESTED:
1. Isolate P-101 per OISD-STD-105 work permit procedure before any intervention.
2. Inspect drive-end and non-drive-end bearings for wear or lubrication failure.
3. Check coupling alignment using laser alignment tool; tolerance 0.05mm.
4. Replace bearing if wear exceeds manufacturer tolerance (ref. OEM manual section 4.2).
5. Verify vibration levels post-repair are within 4.5 mm/s RMS limit before returning to service.

SAFETY PRECAUTIONS:
Work permit to be obtained from Shift In-Charge as per OISD-STD-105. Lockout-tagout (LOTO)
mandatory before opening pump casing. PPE: safety glasses, gloves, steel-toe boots.

WORK COMPLETED:
Date: 14-Mar-2026
Technician: R. Sharma (Employee ID: MT-4521)
Findings: Drive-end bearing showed spalling damage, consistent with lubrication starvation.
Bearing replaced (Part No: SKF-6309-2RS). Coupling realigned to 0.03mm tolerance.
Post-repair vibration: 2.1 mm/s RMS. Equipment returned to service at 16:40 hrs.
